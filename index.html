The Maya Mission

At Maya Pet Co, we believe pet gear should tell a story. Born from my daily work at Maya's Pet Grooming, I saw a need for materials that were as resilient as they were beautiful. This led me back to the ancient textile traditions of the Kathmandu Valley and the high-altitude forests of the Himalayas.
